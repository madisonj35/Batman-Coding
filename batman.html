The Origin of Batman

The story of Batman dates back to Bruce Wayne's childhood. Batman was created out of motivation to avenge his parents death. Bruce was only 8 years old when his parents were murdered in front of him in an alley. Wanting to end all crime in Gotham City so this would never happen again Bruce set out to become a crime fighter. With the help of his butler Alfred Pennyworth, Bruce became what seemed like an unstopabble force in ridding the city of crime.

Bruce Wayne

Bruce Wayne is a billionaire playboy and philanthropist. He spends most of his time when he is not fighting crime leading his company Wayne Tech into the future. Secretly, Wayne Tech provides all of Batman's gadgets that he uses such as smoke bombs, batarangs, and his grapple gun. No one knows his secret identity but his butler, Alfred, and Dr. Leslie Thompkins, his fathers friend.

The Dark Knight

Batman is known for the being "The Dark Knight", he is the caped crusader who saves everyone from the evil of the streets and alleys of Gotham City, it is he who constantly uses his great detective skills to throw off his massive rogues gallery.

Rogues Gallery

Batman possesses and impressive array of villains or "rogues" and the vary from A-list villains to C-listers. Some are absolutely normal humans who have incredible knowledge while others are mutated or may have powers. Nevertheless, Batman always defeats them all.
