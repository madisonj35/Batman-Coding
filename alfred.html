The Story of Alfred Pennyworth

The story of Alfred Pennyworth starts way before the time of Bruce Wayne. Alfred was a butler for the British Royal Family before transitioning over to the Wayne family. Alfred was also previously in the British Guard as well.

Mr. Pennyworth

Alfred Pennyworth has raised Bruce ever since Bruce's parents were murdered many years ago. Coming to his aid Alfred has always been a confidant and mentor. Nevertheless, he still maintains his business relationship with Bruce as his butler, even though Alfred has been a fatherly figure for him.

Best Ally Ever

Alfred Pennyworth being in the British Gaurd has come to be very resourceful for the Batman. Tending to his aid in medicine, and helping with his detective work, Alfred has proven himself many times over to be the best ally for the Batman.
