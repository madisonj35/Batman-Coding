The Origin of Batgirl

Batgirl is a female crime fighter in the city of Gotham. She is Batman's second sidekick and daughter of commisoner Jim Gordon. Like Dick Grayson, Barbara was trained by Bruce Wayne as well. Utlizing all of his weaponry, and following in her fathers footsteps of fighting crime, she makes a wonderful addition to the Batfamily fighting to end crime.

Barbara Gordon

Daughter to the commisioner, Jim Gordon. Barbara began her journey by paying attention to her dad and learning his many detective skills. Wanting to fight crime herself she began crusading the streets of gotham before eventually donning the cape and cowl and becoming the Batgirl as we know today.

The Strategist

Batgirl has highly advanced detective skills compared to the rest of the team and uses this remarkably well. Batgirl eventually became someone behind the scenes and less on the forefront when she was shot by the Joker and became paralyzed. Not wanting to be apart, she worked alongside everyone from the Batcave and gave great insight into helping stop the villains in Gotham City.
