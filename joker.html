The Origin of The Joker

The Joker is homicidal maniac clown who enjoys playfully torturing his victims before killing them. Soemetimes he doe sit for play other times just to get at the Batman, but his crimes usually only makes sense to himself. The Joker loves to playfully taunt at The Batman by leading him on wild goose chases that usually lead to dead ends, just for The Joker to get a laugh.

Jack Napier

The Joker, whose real name is Jack Napier was comedian. Trying his best to find comedic jobs at night clubs and bars, he never got the full swing of it. Proving to not be very funny he began to turn to people who could possibly get him some money. This turned out to be a bad decision as he got caught up with the wrong crowd and fell into an acid like liquid. Permenantley turning him white and disgfiguring his face, The Joker was born and now he was manic and turned to a life of crime.

Batman's Most Formidable Foe

The Joker has been Batman's must enduring villain for the longest of time. Through every opportunity and chance he can get he is often seen tormenting and causing havoc among the villains of Gotham City. The Joker is Batman's opposite has he is energetic, mindless, and does everything without purpose, purely for the sake of his own entertainment or greed.

The Joker and Harley Quinn

The Joker had a sidekick/ love interest who worked beside him. She was known as the Harley Quinn. With an unquestionable love and devotion she along with him schemed the most ridiculous crimes and long plagued the life of The Batman.
